During My High School Year:

During my high school years, I was honoured to be appointed as a school prefect. This role came with a great sense of responsibility, as I was entrusted with maintaining discipline, assisting teachers in managing students, and setting a positive example for my peers. As a prefect, I developed strong leadership, communication, and time management skills while balancing my duties with academic commitments. The experience taught me the importance of teamwork, integrity, and accountability qualities that continue to guide me in both academic and personal pursuits.

During My Matriculation Year:

During my matriculation year at Kolej Matrikulasi Perak (KMPk), I was honoured to be selected to represent my college in various academic and co-curricular activities. One of the highlights was my participation in the 3rd Karnival Kimia Malaysia and the 9th Professor Goh Lai Yoong Perak Inter-School Sixth Form Chemistry Quiz 2022, held at UTAR Kampar. Although I did not win, I gained invaluable exposure and new knowledge in the field of chemistry, and had the opportunity to interact with other passionate students from across the state.

My active involvement did not stop there. I was also selected by my Biology lecturer to represent the Jurusan Sains Komputer (Computer Science stream) in a special field expedition to Cameron Highlands. I was chosen to collect specimens of Calymperes camerunense, a species of moss, for a major laboratory experiment involving all biology students at KMPk. It was an enriching experience that allowed me to explore biodiversity, particularly mosses and ferns, in their natural habitat.

Additionally, I was once again chosen by my Chemistry lecturer to participate in an internal competition, Organoamaze 2023, organised by the Chemistry Unit at KMPk. Although I finished in fourth place, the competition deepened my understanding of organic chemistry and improved my confidence.

I have consistently been selected by my lecturers for various responsibilities, which reflects not only my capability and dedication to learning, but also the strength of my interpersonal skills. I believe my ability to communicate effectively, work well with others, and take initiative made me a trustworthy and dependable candidate in the eyes of my lecturers.

During My Degree Year:

During my first year, I joined my first co-curricular activity, which was theatre. It was completely out of my comfort zone, as acting was never one of my strong suits. However, the experience turned out to be incredibly rewarding. I gained new skills in stage performance, teamwork, and creative expression, while also building confidence through something unfamiliar. As part of the programme, we organised a theatre project where some of us had to act and perform in front of an audience. A new and exciting challenge for me.

In my second semester, I joined another co-curricular activity which is netball. Sports have never been my strongest area, but throughout the semester, I gradually built physical strength and confidence through training and playing. As part of the programme, we were tasked with organising a netball competition, and I took on the role of a sponsorship committee member. I successfully secured a sponsorship from Janzen, which was a proud achievement for me and a valuable learning experience in communication, negotiation, and event coordination.

In my second year, I joined the Software Engineering Club (SEC) and was honoured to be selected as the Head Exco of Publicity and Multimedia. Through this role, I gained valuable experience in leadership, teamwork, and creative design. I learned how to design engaging and professional posters, manage visual content, and contribute to the club’s promotional efforts. This position not only improved my technical and design skills but also boosted my confidence in handling responsibilities and collaborating with others on various club initiatives.

On 17 January 2025, I joined an industrial visit called Tech Pathways: Industry Exploration at the Huawei Customer Solution Innovation Center (CSIC), organised by the Software Engineering Club (SEC). I was part of the multimedia committee, responsible for capturing photos, creating content, and promoting the event. This experience improved my media skills and gave me valuable exposure to real-world technologies used in the industry, especially in a leading global tech company like Huawei.
